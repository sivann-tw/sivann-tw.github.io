  • Ivan Chang CEO

    Ivan Chang co-founded sivann in 2014 and Ivan was appointed as CEO and President. From 2011 to 2014, Mr. Chang held various engineering and marketing positions in LitePoint where he help localize product marketing strategy for cellular and connectivity ATE turnkey solution in Taiwan. Before joining Litepoint, Mr. Chang served as a member of technical staff at Maxlinear in US, where he developed wide experience and contributed knowledge all the way from system design verification stage to mass production testing stage for broadband RFIC & SoC.

    Mr. Chang received an M.S.E.E from the Viterbi School of Engineering, University of Southern California, USA, an E.M.B.A. program certificate from the National Taiwan University, Taiwan.
